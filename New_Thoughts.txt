Battle Wars - Border Skirmish

CR Basic Deck - This deck seeks to hold out against its opponents until it can build its siege cannons to decimate enemy cities
UR Basic Deck - This deck wants to sacrifice its units to destory higher value enemies and attack cities off-guard while they are undefended
FS Basic Deck - This deck plays better than average units and keeps them strong through tactics, strategies, and support units
TA Basic Deck - This deck rushes to overwhelm its opponents early on with cheap infantry until it can begin to pump out strong vehicles

Continental Republic - Focus on Ranged, Tactics, and Structures
United Resistance - Focus on Camo, Infiltration, and AP
Trade Association - Focus on Mechanized, Strategies, and Infantry
Federated States - Focus on Support and Vehicles
